The Power Behind the Progress

Verified Actions, Earned Points

Earn points for tangible green actions: water reuse, public transport, planting, and waste reduction. Our **AI-Assisted Photo Recognition** verifies completion (e.g., checking for specific objects), ensuring fair competition and reliable impact measurement. Individual scores combine for collective family progress.

Track Your True Impact

See real-time progress with interactive tracking graphs. Eliminate guesswork and view actual savings in kWh, liters of water, kg of waste, and **Estimated CO2 Reduction**. This clear impact data reinforces positive habits and provides governments with essential metrics for climate reporting.

Personalized Goals & Knowledge

Set custom weekly/monthly Green Goals (e.g., '10% electricity reduction') with AI guidance for realistic targets based on history. Access curated **Educational Content** (guides, videos) on sustainable shopping, composting, and more, empowering your family with knowledge.
