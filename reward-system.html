Incentives That Drive Action

Hadia uses exciting rewards to maintain high user motivation and engagement. Rewards are directly linked to performance and league progression:

  • **League Progression:** Upon successfully moving up a league (e.g., from Bronze to Silver), families receive a "small gift" such as a **coupon or voucher**.
  • **The Grand Prize:** The overall winner of the highest league (Platinum/Diamond) will win a **huge grand prize**, providing a powerful ultimate goal.

Local Impact & Seamless Donations

Our reward system is designed to benefit the entire community. Rewards (coupons) are issued from **local businesses**, helping the community thrive by driving new, loyal customers to these establishments.

For families who wish to contribute their points differently, a simple **QR code scan at charities automatically logs points**, smoothening the donation process and earning them recognition in the app.
