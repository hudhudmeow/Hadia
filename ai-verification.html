Proof of Green Actions

To ensure the integrity of the Leaderboards and the competition, Hadia leverages simple, smart technology. When a user completes a Green Action, they upload a photograph as proof.

This proof is then analyzed using AI-Assisted Photo Verification to check for common objects related to the action.

Example: For the action "Reused Water," the AI can check the image for objects like a bucket, watering can, or plants, ensuring the action was genuinely completed. This is crucial for accurate point logging and trust.

Building Trust Through Peer Review

In addition to AI, Hadia integrates a social layer of accountability. We utilize Peer Verification where family members and friends can confirm each other's actions.

This dual verification system (AI + Peer) helps build trust within the community, strengthen family bonds, and ensures that the points earned are genuine, making the competition fun and fair for everyone.
