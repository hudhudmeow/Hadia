Turning boring intentions into consistent, lasting eco-friendly habits.

The Problem We Solve

We recognized that for many, adopting eco-friendly habits is boring and unrewarding. People struggle with complexity, find it difficult to be consistent, and often fail to see their real-time impact, leading to low motivation and eventual burnout.

The Hadia Solution

Hadia is a high-quality mobile app prototype designed to bridge the gap between awareness and action. Our solution is simple: Gamify Going Green.

  • Core Goal: Help families reduce energy and water consumption and reuse resources (e.g., reusing water).
  • Mechanism: Members compete against each other to earn individual points, which contribute to a collective Family Score.
  • Deeper Impact: The main focus of Hadia is on sustainability and family bonding, helping families be closer and spend more meaningful moments together.

Our Core Features

Our prototype is built on smart features that ensure engagement and accountability:

  • Dynamic Leagues: Progression through Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum/Diamond ranks.
  • AI-Assisted Verification: Smart tech ensures fairness by verifying actions (like reusing water) via photo proof.
  • Impact Tracking: Visual graphs show actual savings in kWh, liters, and Estimated CO2 Reduction.
  • Win Rewards: Exciting rewards from local businesses for league progression and a huge grand prize for the winner.
