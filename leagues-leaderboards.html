Climb the Ranks: Bronze to Platinum

The core of the Hadia experience is the gamified progression system. Families start in the **Bronze League** and advance by consistently earning points through Green Actions.

  • League Structure: Families progress through distinct tiers: **Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum (or Diamond)**.
  • Promotion: The top-performing families (e.g., the top two) in a league period are upgraded to the next level.
  • Sustained Engagement: This tiered system provides continuous motivation and a sense of achievement, preventing the "burnout" common in flat leaderboards.

Family Score vs. Individual Effort

Hadia encourages both personal responsibility and family teamwork. While individuals have their own accounts and earn points, their scores combine to boost the **Family Score** which determines the League ranking.

Families are also ranked against others in the same city, fostering a healthy, localized community competition and a strong collective push for sustainability goals.
